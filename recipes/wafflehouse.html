Waffle House Special

Now this may not look like much but this recipe is textbook.

Here is what you will need:

  • 2 eggs
  • Shredded hash browns
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tbsp of butter
  • Bacon (optional)
  • Pancake mix (optioal)
  • Jelly (optional)

Follow these steps:

  1. If you are using the pancake/waffle mix start by creating your mixture and letting it sit.
  2. In the meantime, or if you want to skip the pancake/waffle, heat 1 tbsp of butter to the bottom of a pan.
  3. Once butter is melted, put your frozen hashbrowns on the skillet cook for about 10 minues or until the bottom is golden brown, then flip, no stirring. Season to taste.
  4. The hash browns will take some time so plase begin cooking your bacon and/or pancake/waffle. If you do not have pancake mix or bacon skip to step 6.
  5. The hash, bacon, and/or pancake/waffles should be ready at the same tme. Now toast bread and prepare the eggs just how you like them, I go for overeasy.
  6. With no bacon or pancakes just toast bread as you make your eggs.

Thats it! Easy peasy right?
